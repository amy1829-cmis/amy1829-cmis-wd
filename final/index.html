Welcome to Red Velvet Bakery!

We have a wide variety of red velvet cookies, red velvet pastries, red velvet cupcakes, red velvet brownies, and red velvet cakes. We can also deliver your order locally. We request a 2 day notice on most orders since they are made fresh. We can sometimes accommodate a one day order if the items are available.
